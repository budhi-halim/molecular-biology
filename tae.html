TAE Buffer 50Ã— pH 8.0 (1 L)

  1. Dissolve 242 g tris in 500 mL distilled water.
  2. Mix tris and 100 mL EDTA (0.5 M) solution, add 57.1 mL glacial acetic acid.
  3. Adjust pH to 8 by adding concentrated HCl/NaOH. Do not over-adjust.
  4. Adjust volume to 1 L, autoclave.
  5. Dilute to lower concentration as needed.
